About Me

I am a 21-year-old computer science enthusiast hailing from Venice Beach, California, now immersing myself in academia at the University of Michigan. My experience growing up in a locale famed for its dynamic intersection of art and technology has significantly influenced my passion for creating innovative and unique solutions. In the constantly evolving world of technology, I revel in staying at the forefront of new developments. In particular, the emergence of generative AI has captured my interest. Coding for me goes beyond work; it's a creative outlet and a tool to transform abstract thoughts into something powerful. Every line of code acts as a stroke on a canvas, coming together to form an innovative solution never seen before. The thrill of developing new things and venturing into unknown terrains of software design is what drives me, one project at a time.
